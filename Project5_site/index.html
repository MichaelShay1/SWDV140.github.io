Welcome to your lunch stop or night off!

Established in 2009, the Mont Blanc Coffeehouse is a Coffeehouse/Inn along the 165km Tour Du Mont Blanc Trail. We have a rotating selection of roasters and suppliers, and we have rooms availible to rent. Guests must book early because we fill up fast, especially in July and August. We provide breakfast, lunch, and dinner options. Including packed lunches for hikers. We offer spectacular views of Mont Blanc, and the Mont Blanc range. We can book gear transport upon request.
